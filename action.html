✦ How to be supportive ✦

✦  Don't spread stereotypes /  myths about autism.

✦  Do your own reading! (links under  resources page)

✦  Make creations more inclusive in general. For example, you can add subtitles to videos, large fonts / alt text to websites, accurately portrayed autistic characters in your writing, etc.

✦  Don't stare, and understand that many autistic people struggle with eye contact, so don't force people to act differently for you.

✦  Be open; the biggest reason behind the stigma is when people try to avoid the topic due to misconceptions about autism.

✦  Understand that autistic people are not all the same. We are a spectrum; some need a lot of support, and others are completely independent.
